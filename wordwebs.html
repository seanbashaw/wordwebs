WordWebs is a word game where you want to uncover all the hidden words/phrases. Guess one letter at a time or the whole word. Press submit/enter/return to check if your guess is valid. You'll get one word to start: all connections to any word will be highlighted in orange.

This game is still in very early development, and any and all feedback is appreciated. Right now I'm working with MongoDB and Node.js to create a version that can support automatic daily puzzles and level packs.
